Volunteer Requirements

To become a volunteer at the pet shelter, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Complete a volunteer application
  • Pass a background check
  • Attend a volunteer orientation

Volunteer Responsibilities

As a volunteer at the pet shelter, you will have the following responsibilities:

  • Assist with feeding, grooming, and exercising the animals
  • Clean and maintain the shelter facilities
  • Provide socialization and enrichment for the animals
  • Assist with adoption events and fundraising activities

Volunteer Benefits

By volunteering at the pet shelter, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Make a positive impact on the lives of animals in need
  • Gain hands-on experience working with animals
  • Develop new skills and knowledge
  • Join a community of like-minded animal lovers
